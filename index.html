       Our History

Tekton was founded in 1952 in West Lafayette, Indiana by Taylor Tekton. Taylor got his start in the construction industry in 1948 when he joined Steinberger Construction as an apprentice. Taylor possessed great leadership, adaptability, and an unmatched determination for project accuracy and satisfaction. Values we still share at Tekton to this day! Tayler was soon promoted to project liaison at Steinberger, and one year later chose to leave Steinberger to start his own construction company, and alas Tekton was born! Taylor retired from Tekton in 2011 and his daughter Tracy continues on his legacy as our CEO!
         Taylor Tekton

Our People

With over 6,250 employees spread across our organization, we are proud to have each and every one of them be a part of our Tekton family! At Tekton, you have the opportunity to start anywhere in our organization through our award winning apprenticeship programs. Whether that be in our electrical, plumbing, or carpentry divisions, Tekton has a place for you! We also have teams dedicated to our product lines where we offer state of the art construction tools and services. Tekton is committed to the development and advancement of our people from within. We are honored to be able to say that all members of our Executive team once started out in one of our apprenticeship programs!
