Tekton History & About Us



       Our History

Tekton was founded in 1952 in West Lafayette, Indiana by Taylor Tekton. Taylor got his start in the construction industry in 1948 when he joined Steinberger Construction as an apprentice. Taylor possessed great leadership, adaptability, and an unmatched determination for project accuracy and satisfaction. Values we still share at Tekton to this day! Tayler was soon promoted to project liaison at Steinberger, and one year later chose to leave Steinberger to start his own construction company, and alas Tekton was born! Taylor retired from Tekton in 2011 and his daughter Tracy continues on his legacy as our CEO!
         Taylor Tekton

Tekton Timeline

  • 1952 - Taylor founds Tekton in West Lafayette, IN.
  • 1967 - Tekton launches first internally designed and developed tool.
  • 1978 - Tekton establishes partnership with Purdue University for several new campus projects.
  • 1999 - Tekton opens first facility outside of Indiana in Champaign, IL.
  • 2005 - Tekton becomes largest all-service construction firm in the Northwest.
  • 2011 - Taylor steps down from the business as his daughter Tracy advances to be the new CEO
  • 2025 - Tekton announces merger with farming equipment giant Coltivare to enhance the product and services we can provide to our customers.
